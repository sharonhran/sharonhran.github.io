Headquarters: A Smoothie Centre

"Take a cube, gather here, and do more."

Headquarters is a smoothie company designed to promote a place to bond and play. Geared towards young families, parents are able to take a timely break from work or home and easily spend quality time with the family. It does not take much effort to take a trip to the nearest Headquarters to grab a smoothie, excite the kids with the collectible cube beverage containers, and allow their imaginations to spur as they play with these simple, attractive objects. Young adults, parents, and babysitters all the while can enjoy and refuel from a healthy drink.

Container sizes:
Junior, Regular, Family
